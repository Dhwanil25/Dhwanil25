  ## Research Philosophy

  AI systems fail in subtle ways — and most people only notice after deployment.

  I work backwards from failure: identifying breakdown points, designing evaluation
  before building, and treating LLMs as probabilistic reasoning engines rather than
  deterministic truth sources.
